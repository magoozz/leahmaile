My name is Leah! I am an elementry teacher based in Boise, Idaho! I am excited to help capture your special moments from graduations to milestone celebrations, team photos to cherished family portraits, and beyond!

I've been drawn to the camera ever since I was a little girl I spent countless hours planning, producing, and editing photo shoots for my friends. As the years went by I have only become more passionate for being behind the camera. Learning new things and broadening my horizons has done nothing by allow me to explore what I can do when it comes to capturing special moments and bringing them to life in a way that others are able to look back on a lifetime full of memories.

Besides media I find inspiration in my job working as a second grade teacher.
